### 上课服装要求


请按图准备好体服。

最低限度是软底鞋，大袜（运动型紧身裤、legging亦可，但不要穿阔腿裤）和贴身的上衣。

谢谢配合。


### Dress Code in Class

Please dress according to the photo. 

Leotards in any style or color are welcomed, while the tights need to be white or similar.

Thanks for your cooperation.
